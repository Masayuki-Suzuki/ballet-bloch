performance opportunities with professional

In June 2016 Ballet Bloch Canada students shared the stage with six professional dancers: Sophia Lee, Principle of Royal Winnipeg Ballet; Michael Linsmeier, Soloist of Oregon Ballet Theatre; Joshua Beamish, Artist and Choreographer MOVE the company; Mark Dennis, Ballet Kelowna; Joshua Haban, Centre Stage: On Pointe (movie); and Jake Preece, Ballet West II.

Welcome to Ballet Bloch Canada

This year we celebrate our 5th Anniversary and plan for our third annual full-length ballet in June 2017. We continue to offer the best in classical ballet training, contemporary technique and choreography and both Gyrotonic® and Pilates body conditioning specialized for dancers. We start the year with our summer school intensive on 15 August with Stephanie Saland (former Principal Dancer of New York City Ballet) teaching classical technique and variations. We have an exciting year ahead - do join us in creating the future for young dancers in Canada.
